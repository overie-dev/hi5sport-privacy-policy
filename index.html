Hi5Sport – Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly in the app:

  1. Open Hi5Sport
  2. Go to "My Profile"
  3. Scroll down
  4. Tap "Delete my account"
  5. Confirm

All your data will be permanently deleted.

Deleted data includes:

  • Profile
  • Activities
  • Messages
  • Matches

Contact: support@hi5sport.app
