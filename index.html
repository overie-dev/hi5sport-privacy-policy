Hi5Sport – Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly in the Hi5Sport mobile application.

Steps to delete your account:

  1. Open the Hi5Sport app
  2. Go to "My Profile"
  3. Scroll down
  4. Tap "Delete my account"
  5. Confirm the deletion

All your data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

Data deleted includes:

  • User profile information
  • Activities and history
  • Messages and chats
  • Matches and connections

Data retention

No user data is retained after account deletion.
